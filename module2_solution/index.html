Chicken

As the most common type of poultry, chickens are very diverse and can be made into many kinds of dishes. From fried chicken to garlic chicken to chicken curry and many various types, they can be catered even to those with specific preferences regarding food.

Beef

Being the meat of cows and cattles, beef can also be applied to various kinds of dishes. A good source of nutrients and vitamins, beef are commonly mixed with garlic, soy sauce, and pepper to enhance flavors and texture.

Sushi

Sushi, the dish that originated in Japan and has now taken over the world due to its rich and unique combinations, from rice, raw fish and various types of seafood, seaweed, vegetables, and other ingredients. Through the years, sushi has been classified into many types.
